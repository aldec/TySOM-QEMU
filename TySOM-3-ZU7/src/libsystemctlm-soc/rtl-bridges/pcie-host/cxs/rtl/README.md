|S_AXI_DATA_WIDTH              	  |   32              | 32              |
|CXS_DATA_FLIT_WIDTH        		  |  256,512,1024  |   256 ,512,1024         |
|CXS_CNTL_WIDTH             	  |   14,36,44             | 14 ,36,44          |
|CXS_DATACHECK           |0                   | 0           |
|CXS_REPLICATION |0 |0|
|USR_RST_NUM                      | 1 to 4              | 4            |
|LAST_BRIDGE                      |   0 to 1            | 0,1               |
